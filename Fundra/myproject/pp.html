FUNDRA Banking Privacy Policies

1. Information Collection

FUNDRA collects personal and financial information from you when you open an account, perform transactions, or use our online services. This information includes but is not limited to your name, address, Social Security number, account balances, and transaction history.

2. Information Use

The information collected is used to provide and improve our banking services, process transactions, comply with legal requirements, and protect against fraud. We may also use your information to send you notifications about your account and promotional materials.

3. Information Sharing

FUNDRA does not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law, for processing transactions, or with your consent. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business and servicing your account.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and access controls. Despite these efforts, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the complete security of your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience, gather general visitor information, and track visits to our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

6. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. If you need to update or correct your information, please contact our customer service department. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Children's Privacy

FUNDRA does not knowingly collect or maintain information from individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

FUNDRA reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes have been made constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
